Our meticulous launch process for new sites


At Craft CoDe we emphasise using high-quality materials that meet Indian Standard (IS) specifications on every project. This ensures durability and value for your money. Our team, from the backend office to the execution team on-site, works together to deliver exceptional workmanship within your timeframe.
Craft CoDe also values after-sales service and provides prompt attention to all maintenance requests and offers a comprehensive and quality-focused approach to construction projects, ensuring a successful outcome for their clients.
In-depth analysis of drawings and specifications to thoroughly understand the project requirements before starting any work.
Comprehensive site planning by qualified engineers and technicians to ensure a well-defined plan for efficient execution.
Dedicated site coordinators from day one to have a point person on-site to oversee quality control, safety compliance, and adherence to plans.
Strict adherence to Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS) regulations to prioritise workers and public safety by following fire protection and accident prevention protocols.
Close monitoring of timelines and milestones to keep the project on track and ensure timely completion of each stage.
Dedicated back-office support team to ensure materials are delivered to the site on schedule and maintain clear communication with project managers and architects.
